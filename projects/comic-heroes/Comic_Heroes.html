Comic Heroes - a VR JRPG Game

My team created a game called Comic Heroes, a VR JRPG set in a modern fantasy world where magic, monsters, and chaotic supernatural events are treated as part of everyday life. Players experience the story through the perspective of an ordinary person navigating school, relationships, and daily routines while constantly being pulled into unpredictable conflicts and monster encounters. The game blends slice-of-life storytelling with immersive VR combat, allowing players to cast magic and battle creatures as they uncover the larger narrative surrounding their life and the strange world around them.
